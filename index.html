Welcome to Moving Meditation

A lively workshop series for anyone seeking a conscious pause and a way to rediscover the moment.

The practice
Through “moving meditation” joyful techniques that bring balance and cultivate playful lightness when practiced regularly are shared / will be taught. Each session invites you to explore alternative forms of mindfulness — dynamic, curious, and experimental —that reconnect body and mind.

What to expect
A colorful mix of moving meditations, activating and relaxing sequences, inspired by tantric, Sufism, and occupational therapy impulses — playful, profound, and holistic. Together we create a safe space to experiment, express, and simply be.

Participation
To keep it personal and mindful, groups are limited to 7-10 participants
Note: Wear comfortable clothes that allow free movement — no sportswear required.
